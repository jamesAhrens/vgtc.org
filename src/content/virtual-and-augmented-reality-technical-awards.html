The IEEE VGTC Technical Awards program recognizes individuals who have made a significant contribution to the community through their research. The VGTC Technical Awards chair for Virtual and Augmented Reality is Henry Fuchs. VGTC members may nominate individuals by contacting him at vgtc-vr-awards@vgtc.org.
